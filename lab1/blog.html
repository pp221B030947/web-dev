How did I get into the Iqanat Foundation

Mon 7:45p PM 30/01/23

In our small village, the best students were selected through 3 stages of the Aikanat exam, So I got to the decisive stage at stage 3.Then they gathered us together and we went through Karaganda to Astana.There were 5 days.On day 1, we passed the exam for the aikanat school in Borovoye, then there were various seminars and lectures.Thus, I have made new friends from all sides of Kazakhstan.I am grateful to myself that I went there.The main thing is not victory, but participation!

How has KBTU influenced my life?

Mon 12:07 PM 1/02/23

In the first semester I was alone, there were no friends and my studies were also at a low level.But I decided to change in the second semester, I became an extrovert, improved my studies, returned my degree, met everyone and made friends.KBTU is the best university!
